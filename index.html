Sara Leyva

Hello my name is Sara Leyva, and welcome to Leyva Evergreen Yoga!
Over the years and in the hundreds of hours in training, I have come to the conclusion and the practice of using Yoga as a tool to reinvigorate myself and others both mentally and physically.
As I traversed through my yoga journey i was fortunate enough to learn how to truthfully Love myself, which in turn lead me to wanting to share and teach the practice with others.

Carla Gallo

I started practicing with Sara while she was in training. Seeing her grow as a leader has been such a journey. Sara has a unique way to make her students feel special with every practice she leads.Her open heart brings so much positive light and warmth to the room. She has helped me realize that it’s okay to fall, stumble, and bend, because that’s where I am able to find a sense of unending freedom. Her ability to be honest, thoughtful, and humorous draws me to practice. I believe that Sara will touch so many people and I will be there to continue to support her along the way. I’m so blessed to have Sara in my life!

Tara Sanders

I now love yoga because of Sara Leyva. I used to think yoga was dull, superficial, and honestly too hard, which is why I had a negative lens on it. Sara created an environment that allowed me to explore yoga as a part of my life, and not a chore. Her energy, creativity and all-around beautiful spirit creates a space where yoga feels natural - and fun. It’s almost as if by keeping the class and energy light spirited, a zen is created as a result. There’s a difference between taking a yoga class and taking a yoga class with an instructor who was called to teach. Sara is one of those people who was meant to teach. Her ability to honor and respect everyone where they are in their practice and encourage self-love is beautiful. And, she hands down has the best playlists.
